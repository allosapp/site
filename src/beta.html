About the App

The Allos Breath Pacer is a music-guided breath pacer for slow breathing practices like resonance frequency breathing. The app offers a variety of music and animation that can be customized to guide breathing at 4 to 8 breaths per minute. The app can be played in the background with any heart rate or HRV biofeedback app.
