What is Resonance Frequency Breathing?

Resonance Frequency Breathing is a breathing exercise that involves gently breathing at a slow, steady breathing rate, usually between 4.5 - 7 breaths per minute.

Many people report that breathing in this frequency range is helpful for relaxing and balancing the mind and body, helping to alleviate symptoms of stress, and improving overall health and wellbeing.

Research on slow-paced breathing and resonance frequency breathing has demonstrated this breathing method may help alleviate symptoms of some health conditions and improve some biomarkers associated with mental and physical health.
